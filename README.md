## 📖 About Me

I'm **Ian Ochieng Talo**, a passionate software engineer in training at **Zone 01** (part of the 01 Edu network). I'm learning everything from scratch through Zone 01's unique peer-to-peer methodology - no teachers, no lectures, just pure problem-solving and collaboration.

**What Makes Zone 01 Different:**
* 🤝 **Peer-to-Peer Learning:** Students teach each other through code reviews and collaboration.
* 🛠️ **Project-Based:** Every concept is learned through hands-on projects.
* ⏳ **Self-Paced:** No strict deadlines, encouraging deep understanding and time management.
* 🌍 **Real-World Skills:** From C programming to full-stack development, DevOps, and AI.
